About United Engineering Services

Excellence in Precision Engineering Since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010, United Engineering Services has evolved from a small workshop into a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility, committed to delivering precision components that meet the highest standards of quality and reliability.

Our team of skilled engineers and technicians work tirelessly to ensure that every component we produce meets exacting specifications and exceeds customer expectations.

We continuously adopt cutting-edge technologies to optimize our operations and enhance client services, staying at the forefront of the rapidly evolving industrial landscape.

Our Mission

To provide innovative and reliable precision engineering solutions that exceed customer expectations.

Our Vision

To become the preferred partner for precision machinery components globally.

Our Values

Quality

We are committed to excellence in every component we produce.

Integrity

We conduct our business with honesty and transparency.

Customer Focus

We prioritize understanding and meeting our customers' needs.

Innovation

We continuously seek better ways to serve our customers through technological advancement.

Our Journey

2010

United Engineering Services was founded with a small team of engineers and a workshop in Bangalore.

2013

Expanded operations and moved to a larger manufacturing facility to accommodate growing demand.

2016

Implemented advanced CNC machinery to enhance precision and production capacity.

2019

Began serving international clients and established a reputation for high-quality precision components.

2022

Initiated digital transformation to optimize internal processes and enhance customer experience.

2025

Continuing to grow and innovate, with a focus on sustainable manufacturing practices and expanding our product range.
