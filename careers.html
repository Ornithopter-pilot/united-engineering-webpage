Our Hiring Process

At United Engineering, we've designed our recruitment process to identify candidates who not only have the right skills and experience but also align with our values of excellence, innovation, and collaboration. Our process may vary slightly depending on the department and role.

1

Application Review

Our recruitment team carefully reviews your application, resume, and cover letter to assess your qualifications and experience.

1-2 weeks

2

Initial Screening

A phone or video call with our HR team to discuss your background, experience, and interest in the role.

30-45 minutes

3

Technical Assessment

In-depth discussion with department heads and technical specialists focused on your technical knowledge and problem-solving abilities.

1-2 hours

4

Final Interview

Meet with senior management to discuss your fit with our team, values, and company culture.

45-60 minutes

5

Offer & Onboarding

If selected, you'll receive a competitive offer and begin our comprehensive onboarding program.

Offer within 1 week
